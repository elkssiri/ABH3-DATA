# ABH3-DATA

This repository contains all materials and data used in the study:

"Machine-Learning-Accelerated High-Throughput Screening of Perovskite-Type Metal Hydrides for Solid-State Hydrogen Storage."

## Repository Contents

- **Data_All.odt**  
  Contains all data generated using the trained MEGNet and M3GNet models, including:  
  - Composition  
  - Formation energy (MEGNet, eV/atom)  
  - Lattice parameter (a) predicted by M3GNet  
  - Gravimetric capacity (wt%)

- **DATA_DFT.odt**  
  Contains DFT-computed data, including:  
  - Composition  
  - Formation energy (MEGNet prediction)  
  - Formation energy (DFT)  
  - Lattice parameter (a) predicted by M3GNet  
  - Gravimetric capacity (wt%)

- **Exploration.ods**  
  Contains DFT calculations for ABH3 compounds predicted to be thermodynamically stable by the MEGNet model.  
  This file includes three sheets:

  1. **Sheet 1:**  
     Compositions predicted as stable by MEGNet  
     - Final energy (Ry) ABH3 (DFT)  
     - Formation energy ABH3 (DFT)  
     - Final energy (Ry) AB (CsCl-type)  
     - Formation energy AB (CsCl-type)

  2. **Sheet 2:**  
     ABH3 compounds confirmed as thermodynamically stable by DFT  
     - Final energy (Ry) ABH3 (DFT)  
     - Formation energy ABH3 (DFT)  
     - Final energy (Ry) AB (CsCl-type)  
     - Formation energy AB (CsCl-type)

  3. **Sheet 3:**  
     Thermodynamically stable AB (CsCl-type) compounds and corresponding hydrogenation enthalpy  
     - Final energy (Ry) ABH3 (DFT)  
     - Formation energy ABH3 (DFT)  
     - Final energy (Ry) AB (CsCl-type)  
     - Formation energy AB (CsCl-type)  
     - Hydrogenation enthalpy

These materials are provided to ensure transparency, reproducibility, and reuse of the results presented in the publication.
